Mission Statement

NCGreen seeks to raise awareness about the declining state of our natural world as climate change and global warming continue to progress unchecked. Our community strives to reconnect people with nature and inspire action and collective change.

Meet Our Members

Alicia Slater

As a part of the NCGREEN team, Alicia has been instrumental in driving awareness about the importance of environmental conservation and sustainable living practices. In addition, her expertise in information science has been valuable in supporting the organization to leverage technology to disseminate crucial environmental information and engage with a broader audience. Alicia is an avid nature fanatic who spends as much time as possible exploring the great outdoors. She believes that experiencing nature firsthand is one of the best ways to appreciate its beauty and understand the importance of preserving it for future generations. Alicia is also an active local community member and regularly participates in environmental initiatives and clean-up efforts. Her dedication to environmental preservation is rooted in her belief that everyone has a responsibility to do their part in protecting the planet. Through her work with NCGREEN and her commitment to the environment, Alicia is helping create a more sustainable and environmentally conscious future for all.

Izzie Estes

Izzie is an active member of NCGREEN committed to learning and educating about responsible interaction with the environment. A passionate supporter of local agriculture, equitable and conscientious food systems, community building, the preservation of natural spaces, and more, Izzie recognizes the intersections between these ideas and the importance of working together to make our world a better place. Having grown up on the Outer Banks of North Carolina, Izzie has felt a deep connection with nature since she was young, and hopes to continue to demonstrate her appreciation of it in the work she does in her community and beyond.

Kendall Williams

Kendall's role as a member of NCGREEN is as a functional marketer of sorts. Her goal has been to increase digital awareness of both the function of NCGREEN and of broad sustainability practices. While environmental conservancy has been a recent focus, she has always had a growing concern for the state of our Earth and hopes to bridge her experiences with digital marketing to her passion for saving and restoring nature. In her free time, Kendall loves to take a blanket and a book and lay outside in the sun.
